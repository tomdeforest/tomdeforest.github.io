About

初めまして！

I’m Tom DeForest. I believe in the power of small and simple things to change my life. In fact, I believe that all meaningful change in the world begins from the little seeds of improvement that we sow in our own lives. On their own, these simple actions can do very little good. But when we consistently make small improvements every day, the compounding effect of those changes moves mountains, touches hearts, and changes lives forever.

I Believe in God

God reaches out and touches my life in so many small and simple ways. Through the divine love of His Son Jesus Christ, I have found enduring peace and happiness.

I am the ninth great-grandson of Jesse the Walloon. In celebration of the 397th anniversary of the arrival of the Walloons in New Amsterdam, I am publishing A Walloon Family in America as an ePub. It is a wonderful tribute and an insightful view into the settling of what is now New York City.
