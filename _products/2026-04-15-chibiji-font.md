Chibiji is a soft, bouncy display typeface, inspired by Japanese kawaii culture. Rounded forms, squishy proportions, and a handcrafted softness make it a natural fit for character brands, packaging, stationery, stickers, pastry shops, baby goods, and anything else that lives in that sweet spot between playful and polished.

The name says it all: chibi (cute, small) + ji (character, glyph).

V1.1 includes:

A new Solid version - great for creating heavy outlines for dramatic effect!

Support for 200+ languages! Includes characters and diacritics for Western European, Central European, Southeastern European, South American, and Esperanto.

Future versions might include:

Alternate glyphs, Oceanian, Pan-African, Vietnamese, and Japanese Kana support.
